## [](#why-choose-gem-wallet-2)Why Choose Gem Wallet?

When it comes to choosing a crypto wallet, you need to consider several factors:

*   **Security:** Gem Wallet prioritizes the safety of your funds. It employs advanced encryption protocols and follows best practices to ensure that your digital assets are protected against unauthorized access.
    
*   **Simplicity:** Gem Wallet is designed with beginners in mind. Its intuitive interface and straightforward navigation make it easy for anyone to start using cryptocurrencies without feeling overwhelmed.
    
*   **Multi-Currency Support:** Gem Wallet supports a wide range of cryptocurrencies, including Bitcoin (BTC), Ethereum (ETH), Binance Coin (BNB) Telegram (TON), and [many others](https://gemwallet.com/assets). You can conveniently manage all your digital assets in one place.
    
*   **Wallet that does not track you:** Gem Wallet allows you to send and receive cryptocurrencies with just a few taps on your smartphone. No tracking of your transactions or anything you do with your wallet.
    
*   **Fiat Onramp:** Buy Crypto with a credit card in just a few taps. No more hassles, only smooth transactions. Download today and step into the future of investing!

### [](#setting-up-a-toncoin-wallet-4)Setting Up a Toncoin Wallet

To get started with TON, download the Gem Wallet, create a new account or import one with a seed phrase, and you’re ready to interact with the TON network. This is your access point to the TON blockchain, enabling you to store, send, and receive Toncoin.

Gem Wallet already has support for Jettons. Learn more here:  
[What Is Jetton and What You Need to Know About Tokens on TON Blockchain](https://gemwallet.com/learn/what-is-jetton-and-what-you-need-to-know-about-tokens-on-the-ton-blockchain)

### [](#does-gem-wallet-hold-my-funds-5)Does Gem Wallet Hold My Funds?

No funds are held on the Gem Wallet app. Gem Wallet randomly generates the keys (Secret Recovery Phrase) that allows you to access funds on the blockchain. These keys unlocks your crypto assets. And it is only given to you upon the creation of the wallet. No one from the Gem Wallet team knows the keys to your wallet.

### [](#is-gem-wallet-a-cryptocurrency-exchange-6)Is Gem Wallet a Cryptocurrency Exchange?

Gem Wallet is not a Cryptocurrency Exchange, instead, it is a bridge that connects you to DeFi platforms that lets you trade, stake and/or participate with permissionless onchain protocols.

The possibilities are endless with Gem Wallet. Your funds are secure on the blockchain and you will have access to it 24/7. No centralized entity will have control over your wallet and you can freely transact with it at all times.

## [](#gem-wallet-compatibility-and-device-support-7)Gem Wallet Compatibility and Device Support

### [](#apple-devices-8)Apple Devices

Gem Wallet can be installed on any of the following Apple Devices:

*   iOS device that is running iOS version 17.0 or later.
    
*   iPadOS Devices that is running iPadOS version 17.0 or later.
    
*   macOS Devices with [Apple silicon](https://support.apple.com/en-us/116943) running macOS version 14.0 or later.
    

Download Gem Wallet for Apple devices from the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/gem-wallet-crypto-bitcoin/id6448712670).

### [](#android-devices-9)Android Devices

Gem Wallet can be installed on Android devices running at least [Android 9.0 (Pie) API level 28](https://developer.android.com/about/versions/pie/android-9.0).

Download Gem Wallet for Android devices from the [Google Play Store](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.gemwallet.android) or download and install this [APK](https://apk.gemwallet.com/gem_wallet_latest.apk).
