*   Choose Your Migration Path & Update Your Code
*   Based on your current SDK, follow the appropriate migration instructions
*   From [@ton](/u/ton)/ton:

```

const client = new ToncoreAdapter({
network: "mainnet",
apiKey: "YOUR_API_KEY",
});
```

